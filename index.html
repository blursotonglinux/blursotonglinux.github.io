A Small Note on the Name:

Leaning into the Singlish phrase blur like sotong, the name BLUR playfully embraces that initial feeling of absolute cluelessness when a rookie first steps into the Linux playground.
Behind the tongue-in-cheek title, however, lies a genuine mission to introducing youths and adults from non-tech backgrounds to the world of Linux and its open-source playground.

Partner

Partner With Us

We're free and non-commercial, running entirely on volunteer time and generous partnerships. To keep our sessions going, we look for partners who share our equitable and whimsical view of technology.
